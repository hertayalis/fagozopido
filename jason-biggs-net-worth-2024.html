Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jason Biggs was born in Pompton Plains, New Jersey, and showed an interest in acting from a young age. He made his acting debut in the television series ‘Drexell’s Class’ in 1991. However, his breakthrough came in 1999 when he starred in the comedy film ‘American Pie.’

Biggs’ portrayal of Jim Levenstein in ‘American Pie’ was a critical and commercial success, leading to several sequels. His performance in these films significantly boosted his popularity and wealth.

Other Notable Roles
