Tiki Barber Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Tiki Barber, the former professional American Football player with the New York Giants, has a net worth estimated at $18 million, according to recent sources. Barber was inducted into the Virginia Sports Hall of Fame in 2011 and he has added to his net worth as a sports correspondent and host of various events, as well as through his writing.

Barber, born on April 7, 1975, in Blacksburg, Virginia, played professionally from 1997 to 2006 and set multiple records as the Giants’ all-time leader in rushing attempts, rushing yards, and rushing touchdowns. His football career earnings totaled $35 million.

In addition to his sports career, he has pursued various ventures to increase his wealth. He has worked as a television broadcaster, including on NBC Sports programs “Today” and “Football Night in America/Sunday Night Football,” co-hosted programs on television and radio such as “Fox & Friends” on the Fox News Channel and the weekly show “Barber Shop” on Sirius Satellite Radio.

Barber has also authored books, including his autobiography “Tiki: My Life and the Game Beyond” and the intense lifting program “Tiki Barber’s Pure Hard Workout.” He has also co-authored multiple children’s books with his twin brother, Ronde Barber.

Barber’s personal life has been the subject of public interest, having been married to Ginny Cha until 2012 and later marrying NBC intern Traci Lynn Johnson. The couple resides in New York City.

Structurally, Tiki Barber’s net worth includes a diverse set of elements, including not just his football career but also his roles as a sports correspondent, analyst, and his ventures in broadcasting, writing, and public appearances. While his personal life has been a matter of public scrutiny, these professional pursuits continue to contribute to his overall net worth.
