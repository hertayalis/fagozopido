Who is Mike Norvell Wife? A Look at His Relationship Status

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Mike Norvell is an American college football coach. He’s the head coach at Florida State. Before that, he led the team at Memphis and coached at various other places. In his college days, he played as a wide receiver at the University of Central Arkansas.

However, there is a curiosity among his fans related to his personal life. They want to know with whom Mike Norvell is married. In this post, you will get all the information related to it.

Who is Mike Norvell Wife?

Mike Norvell’s wife is Maria Norvell, previously known as Maria Chiolino. The couple first met during a ballroom dancing class at the University of Central Arkansas, where Maria was pursuing her master’s degree.

Mike, a football player and star wide receiver at the time, paired up with Maria in the class, marking the beginning of their relationship. They married in 2004 and have since shared a strong bond, with Maria playing a significant role in Mike’s career decisions and transitions.

Mike Norvell posted a picture wishing his wife a happy birthday, along with a caption,

“Happy Birthday to the most amazing woman I have ever known, she inspires me to be better everyday. Such an incredible wife, mother, and friend who I am grateful to be on this life long journey with. Through the ups and downs she is always there so supportive. Happy Birthday Maria”

How Many Children Does Mike Norvell Have?

Mike Norvell and his wife Maria have one child, a daughter named Mila, who was born on June 23, 2014. Mila, now eight years old, is the couple’s only child.

Mike Norvell posted a picture with his daughter Mila and included a caption,
