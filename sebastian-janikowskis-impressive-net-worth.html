How much is Sebastian Janikowski worth? His net worth is estimated to be $14 Million

Sebastian Janikowski: A Brief Biography

Born on March 2, 1978, in Wałbrzych, Poland, Sebastian Janikowski is renowned as a placekicker for the Oakland Raiders of the National Football League (NFL). His powerful kicking ability has earned him the nickname “The Polish Cannon.”

Sebastian’s net worth as of mid-2016 is estimated to be $14 million, derived from his successful career as a football player. His annual salary amounts to $4.75 million.

Sebastian Janikowski’s Earnings and Career

Sebastian’s father, Henryk, a former soccer player, moved to the USA in the 1980s, enabling Sebastian to join him after his parents’ divorce. After displaying exceptional soccer skills, Sebastian transitioned to American Football and excelled as a placekicker.

His high school accomplishments led to scholarships from prestigious universities, ultimately choosing Florida State University. At Florida State, he continued to shine, winning two Lou Groza Awards and earning Consensus All-American honors twice.

His professional career officially commenced in 2000 when he was selected by the Oakland Raiders as the 17th overall pick in the NFL Draft. During his 17-season career, Sebastian achieved numerous records, including being the Raiders’ all-time leading scorer, and securing contracts that augmented his net worth.

Married to Lori, with twin girls, Sebastian keeps a low profile regarding his personal life, despite being active on social media, particularly Twitter.
