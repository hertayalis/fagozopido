Jennifer Lopez Wears Knee-High Faux Fur Stiletto Boots on Insta

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

J.Lo is our eternal style icon, and her latest photo shoot proves why.

In a stunning snap to promote her JLo x DSW footwear line, Jennifer Lopez can be seen wearing an oversized teddy coat, with a low-cut, see-through top featuring buttons down the front. Forgoing pants, J.Lo wears a pair of her own JLo x DSW "Forca boots," and she most definitely has our attention with this look.

With her hair worn down in loose curls, and sporting a dark red lip, Lopez looks practically perfect in every way.

While Lopez's faux fur stiletto boots most definitely caught our attention, they weren't the only impeccable look that the "In the Morning" singer served this week. While accepting the icon award at the 2020 Billboard Women in Music event on Thursday, Lopez looked seriously stylish in a knitted cream cardigan. She wore her hair up, but kept two blonde tendrils down by her face. Her skin was positively glowing, accentuated by a light pink lip gloss, and a shimmery eye shadow.

During her appearance, Lopez gave sweet shoutouts to her fianc√©, Alex Rodriguez, and the couple's blended family. "Of course I want to thank my beautiful family for going on the road with me, for touring with me, for supporting me, for allowing me to be the artist that I am," she said. "Alex and our four beautiful kids, thank you so much, I love you, everything I do is for you guys."
