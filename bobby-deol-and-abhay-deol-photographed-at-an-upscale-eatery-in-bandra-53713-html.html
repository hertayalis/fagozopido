Bobby Deol and Abhay Deol photographed at an upscale eatery in Bandra

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 26, 2024
Last night, Bobby Deol and Abhay Deol were snapped at a posh restaurant in Bandra. The two were wearing blue t-shirts of similar shades and trousers. Bobby and Abhay happily posed for the shutterbugs. Bobby was most recently seen in Love Hostel, alongside Vikrant Massey and Sanya Malhotra. Following that, the actor will appear in the third instalment of his much-anticipated web series, Aashram. Bobby will also begin work on Apne 2 soon, in which he will reunite with his father Dharmendra and brother Sunny Deol. Abhay was most recently seen in the 2021 film Velle. He also appeared in web series such as the sci-fi thriller JL50 and the intense 1962: The War in the Hills.
