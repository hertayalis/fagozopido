Miley Cyrus Boyfriend: The Musician Who Captured Her Heart

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Miley Cyrus and her boyfriend, musician Maxx Morando, are dressed to the nines. The two first became involved in December 2021, when they were caught getting close backstage during the singer’s NBC show Miley’s New Year’s Eve Party, holding hands and dancing to the music.

Cyrus was previously spotted with Morando at the Gucci “Love Parade” fashion show in Los Angeles in November 2021, when she posted a photo of them together on Instagram.

Cyrus and Morando made another public appearance together in March 2023 at the Versace presentation in Los Angeles, and the “Flowers” singer recently gave him a special shoutout when she won record of the year at the Grammys. Here’s everything you need to know about Morando and Cyrus.

Who is Maxx Morando?

Maxx Morando is a brilliant musician known for his drumming abilities and production work. He currently performs as the drummer for the rock band Liily, demonstrating his rhythmic ability. Morando previously played drums for the punk rock band The Regrettes in 2015 and 2018. Aside from drumming, he has made important contributions as a producer.

Notably, Morando worked as a producer on Miley Cyrus’ Grammy-nominated album, “Endless Summer Vacation.” He was a significant producer on tracks like “Handstand” and co-produced “Violet Chemistry.”

Morando’s flexibility as a drummer and producer demonstrates his adaptability and competence in the music industry. His wide musical journey and collaborations highlight his considerable effect and contributions to many projects, establishing him as a prominent player in the world of modern music.

He’s younger than Miley Cyrus

Morando was born in 1998, six years younger than Cyrus, but their actual birthdays are fairly close. Morando is a Scorpio born on November 16, while Cyrus is a Sagittarius born on November 23.

He Plays Multiple Instruments

Morando is well renowned for his drumming, but he also plays guitar, as demonstrated in this Instagram Live video from 2020. Morando is presently the drummer for the band Liily, but he formerly drummed for the Regrettes from 2015 until 2018.

He Met Miley Cyrus on a Blind Date

In a cover story interview with British Vogue, the “Flowers” singer explained how she met the fellow musician.

“We got put on a blind date,” Cyrus explained. “Well, it was blind for me and not really for him,” she explained to the site. “I thought, ‘The worst that can happen is I leave.'”

He’s Also a Fashion Designer

During an interview with Vogue in September, Cyrus revealed that Morando collaborated with designer Shane Kastl on one of her festival looks.

“This look is a one-of-a-kind collaboration between two of my favorite emerging artists, Maxx Morando and Shane Kastl,” she explained to the journal about one of her looks. “Their collaboration is the perfect example of sustainability becoming the next fashion phase, and it proves that anything can be reinvented, which resonates with me deeply.”

He’s Been Linked to Miley Cyrus Since 2021

Though the couple originally generated dating rumors in December 2021, they made their first public appearance together at the Gucci “Love Parade” fashion show in Los Angeles alongside Morando in November 2021. In December, the two were spotted getting close during Miley’s New Year’s Eve Party in Miami.
