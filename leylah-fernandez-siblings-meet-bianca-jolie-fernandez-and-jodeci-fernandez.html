Leylah Fernandez siblings: Meet Bianca Jolie Fernandez and Jodeci Fernandez

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Leylah Fernandez is a Canadian professional tennis player. She has a career-high ranking of No. 13 by the Women’s Tennis Association (WTA), achieved on 8 August 2022.

Leylah Fernandez bio

Fernandez is a fan of Spanish football team Real Madrid, and English football team Manchester City.[31] She speaks fluent English, French and Spanish.

The birthplace of Fernandez is Montreal, Quebec. Jorge, her father, is an ex-soccer player who is from Ecuador. Irene, her mother, is a Filipino Canadian. Bianca Jolie, her younger sister, is a tennis player as well.

Leylah Fernandez professional career

Fernandez defeated fellow Canadian Carson Branstine in the Gatineau Challenger final on July 21, 2019, to win her maiden professional singles tennis title.

When Fernandez paired up with Rebecca Marino of Vancouver, they won Fernandez’s first professional doubles championship on the same day.

The pair defeated the second-seeded combo of Taiwanese Hsu Chieh-yu and Mexican Marcela Zacaras. The following week, she competed in her second straight ITF final in Granby, losing to Australian Lizette Cabrera.

Leylah Fernandez siblings, Bianca Jolie Fernandez

Leylah Fernandez has two sisters whose names are Bianca and Jolie Fernandez and Jodeci Fernandez are the two siblings. Bianca Jolie Fernandez and Jodeci Fernandez are celebrity siblings who happen to be the sisters of Leylah Fernandez.

They were all born to the same parents – father Jorge who is from Ecuador and is a former football player, and mother Irene who is a Filipino Canadian.

Bianca Jolie Fernandez is the younger sister of Leylah Fernandez. She is also a Canadian tennis player who has a career-high WTA singles ranking of 891 achieved on 7 March 2022. She also has a career-high WTA doubles ranking of 735 achieved on 7 March 2022

Jodeci Fernandez on the other hand is the elder of Leylah Fernandez. She is the first child and daughter of her parents. She is a dentist.
