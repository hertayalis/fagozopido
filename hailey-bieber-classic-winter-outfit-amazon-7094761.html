Recreate Hailey Biebers Winter Outfit From Amazon

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

On those days when you can’t quite figure out what to wear, it’s helpful to have a go-to outfit in the back of your mind. Even style icon Hailey Bieber has a tried and true outfit recipe, which consists of an oversized leather jacket, simple top or sweater, baggy jeans, and chunky loafers. And we found tons of styles on Amazon you can put together to channel Bieber’s classic winter look for less. 

Over the past couple of months, Bieber has worn her go-to outfit countless times, swapping out each piece for slightly different styles. She has thrown on straight-leg jeans and statement loafers with a leather bomber jacket, oversized aviator-style coat, and even a leather trench. Below, browse through the different Amazon pieces you’ll need to recreate Bieber’s cold weather uniform. 

Shop now: $80 (Originally $90); amazon.com

Shop Faux Leather Jackets:

The star of the show is a faux leather jacket, and Amazon has tons of trendy styles for less than $100. For a sophisticated look, go with this vegan leather blazer from The Drop that comes in four colors and sizes XXS through 6X; it’s “extremely flattering” and “feels so high quality,” according to two happy customers. You can also take the bomber jacket route with this Levi’s collared style that features an elastic waistband, side pockets, zippered cuffs, and a zipper closure down the front. And to go full-on Hailey Bieber, treat yourself to this long, faux leather jacket that’ll instantly elevate your outfit and give you that cool, model-off duty vibe. 

Shop now: $8 (Originally $12); amazon.com

Shop Simple Tops and Sweaters: 

The next piece you’ll need is a simple top or sweater to throw on under the jacket. For just $8, you can get the relaxed-fit Hanes T-shirt a shopper called the “perfect basic tee.” If you prefer a cropped silhouette, opt for this Verdusa tight-fitting top that shows off just the right amount of skin when paired with high-waisted jeans. And if you live in a colder climate, go with this Amazon Essentials crewneck sweater for some extra warmth. It’s made from 100 percent cotton and comes in 20 colors and sizes XS through 6X. 

Shop now: $89 (Originally $108); amazon.com

Shop Straight-Leg Jeans:

Now it’s time to pick out a pair of jeans. Of course, Levi’s has many great options, including these loose, high-waisted ones and classic Ribcage Straight Ankle Jeans. They both have tons of rave reviews and come in a few washes, ensuring you’ll find a pair that matches your style. If you already have baggy jeans and are looking for something a little different, check out this mid-rise pair from The Drop. The light-wash jeans have frayed hems and subtle side slits for extra fashionable flair. 

Shop now: $80 (Originally $99); amazon.com 

Shop Chunky Loafers: 

The final ingredient in this supermodel-inspired outfit is a pair of chunky loafers. Franco Sarto makes cute, customer-loved models, like these faux patent leather crinkle-style ones and this black and brown pair with tassels. They both have thick lug soles that achieve a rugged, oversized look. These Circus by Sam Edelman loafers also make a great choice, as they have elevated design details, like a faux croc finish and a gold buckle, that’ll instantly dress up your jeans and jacket.
