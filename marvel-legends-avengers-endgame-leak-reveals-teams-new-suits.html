Another Avengers: Endgame Leak Reveals The Teams New Suits

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

While the various merchandise for Avengers: Endgame won’t always be an accurate reflection of the film’s actual content, Earth’s Mightiest Heroes have been seen wearing the same white suits in so many toy leaks now that it’s becoming almost impossible to doubt that the outfits will show up in the movie itself.

Recommended Videos

The latest in a growing body of evidence that the so-called “Quantum Realm suits” will feature in the Avengers: Infinity War sequel is a leak from Hasbro’s Marvel Legends line of action figures. In the new image, we see both Black Widow and Hawkeye sporting the new getups, along with a selection of heads belonging to Nebula, Ant-Man and Iron Man, which can presumably be switched onto the bodies.

These five heroes aren’t the only characters who might be wearing the white suits in the upcoming film. Thor, Rocket, and Captain America, for instance, have all been seen in previous toy leaks dressed in the same set of outfits. As for the purpose of these costumes, that remains to be seen, but at the moment, the most popular theory by a wide margin is that the getups will be used for journeys into the Quantum Realm, potentially using this mysterious place as a means of traveling through time.

For all the merchandise leaks that the white suits have shown up in, Marvel Studios has yet to share any footage that actually shows the gang wearing these uniforms. Perhaps they’re saving the reveal for the next Avengers: Endgame trailer, which, contrary to recent speculation, is currently looking unlikely to drop during the Academy Awards. Then again, we may not see the team in their new outfits until the film hits theaters on April 26th, 2019 – which really isn’t too far off now.
