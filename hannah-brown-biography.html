Hannah Brown Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Who is Hannah Brown?

Hannah Brown is a former beauty pageant titleholder as Miss Alabama USA 2018. She also competed in Miss USA 2018.

As of 2019, she is a contestant of the 23rd season of The Bachelor and 28th season of Dancing with the Stars.

Who are the parents of Hannah Brown?

Hannah Kelsey Brown was born on 24th September 1994 in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, US. She is the daughter of Robert Walker-Brown and Susanne Brown.

She was raised both in Tuscaloosa and in nearby Northport, Alabama with her brother Patrick Brown.

Hannah graduated from Tuscaloosa County High School in Northport, Alabama, United States.

In 2017, she graduated from the University of Alabama Tuscaloosa, Alabama with a degree magna cum laude from the University’s College of Communication and Information Sciences.

Similarly, she was a member of the Alpha Upsilon Chapter of the Alpha Chi Omega sorority during college.

Hannah Brown- Professional Career

Beauty Pageants

Hannah Brown began her took part in a beauty pageant contest at the age of 15 by competing in local Alabama teen pageants. She was the first runner-up in Miss Alabama’s Outstanding Teen 2010 and second runner-up in Miss Alabama Teen USA 2011.

Later, she competed in Miss Alabama 2013 representing Tuscaloosa. She returned in 2017 Miss Alabama USA.

Again in 2018, she competed for Miss Alabama USA representing West Alabama and was crowned by Smith.

Likewise, she also competed in the 2018 Miss USA.

Reality-TV Shows

In the year 2018, she was cast in the 23rd season of The Bachelor, where she was placed in seventh place.

Similarly, on 12th March 2019, she became the lead for the upcoming fifteenth season of the show for its second part.

Dancing with the Stars

In August 2019, it was announced that Hannah would be one of the celebrity contestants to compete in the 28th season of Dancing with the Stars.

She was the second woman to participate in Dancing with the Stars on The Bachelorette. Her partner is Alan Bersten.

They are successful in securing a place in the semi-finale of the show.

Awards

In 2019, she won the People’s Choice Awards for The Competition Contestant in The Bachelorette.

Hannah Brown- Net Worth, Salary

Her net worth is around $1.5 million US as of 2023. From Miss Alabama, she received $100,000 US cash scholarships. From Dancing with the Stars, she made around $125,000 US.

How tall is Hannah Brown?

Hannah Brown is a green-eyed blonde. Similarly, she is 5 feet 7 inches tall and weighs around 59 kg.
